ABOUT US

Coffice is a coworking space located at downtown Užice. Bright, comfortable and innovative space that stimulates work focus and creativity is dedicatedly designed for independent workers including freelancer, designers, developers, writers, artists, entrepreneurs and more.

OUR SERVICES

SHARED WORK SPACE

Comfy and flexible working desks are designed for work effectiveness. The open space encourages communication and sharing between co-workers. Right volume of background music, beautiful surrounding views are perfect for work & relaxation balance.

FREE COFFE & TEA

High-quality ground coffee and tea with a wide range of selection that suits any taste are offered free.

HIGH SPEED INTERNET

High speed Wifi is well-prepared with high capacity available and bandwidth balancing modem.

MEETING ROOM

Fully equipped with big screen, and presentation materials – all amenities that you have when meeting in professional office environment

PRINTER/SCANNER/COPIER

Conveniently bring out your idea through printing just one step away from your seating.

LIBRARY

A wide selection of books for your relaxation & knowledge boost.
